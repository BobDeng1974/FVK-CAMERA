Links to Pre-Build FVK-CAMERA projects for MS Visual Studio 2013 and 2015.

Compiled libs of FVK-CAMERA (v1.1.0), FLE (v1.3.4), FLTK (v1.4.x-r12189), OpenCV (v3.2.0), and GLEW (v2.0.0) are already configured in the project.

Simply extract, open the .sln file, build and run.
